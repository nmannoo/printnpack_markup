Print n Pack Ltd is a member of Deloffice Group, the leading importer and distributor of paper and office supplies in Mauritius. The group is willing to extend its activities into printing and packaging as part of its growth strategy. Print n Pack Ltd has been incorporated for this purpose and the company has started operation in March 2018.

We provide a wide range of printing services mainly to satisfy office and operation needs. Printing products include business cards, envelopes, letterheads, complimentary cards, "auto-carbon carnets", hard cover books, forms, questionnaires, posters, leaflets and brochures. We differentiate ourselves through quality and service. Orders and inquiries are handled by our team of professional graphic designers who can assist in artwork conception and design.

We aim to offer a wide range of packaging products to bakeries, pastries, hotels, restaurants, shops, supermarkets and factories.

Target products include:

  • Cake boards, Cake boxes, Cake bags, Plates, Cups, Bowls, Trays, Take Aways, Cling film and Aluminium foil
  • Shopping bags, Gift bags, Gift boxes and Wrapping paper.
  • Shopping bags, Gift bags, Gift boxes and Wrapping paper.
